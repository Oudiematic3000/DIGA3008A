About Me.

Hi there! I'm Ari. I'm a student at the University of Witwatersrand in South Africa. I make games and music. I also make spoofs of albums I like by photoshopping myself onto the covers. Occasionally, I also make jokes. Watch out for those. Promise it'll be worth it.

I love to solve problems creatively, be they anywhere on the spectrum from unique sorting algorithms to figuring out you can put hot dogs in the air fryer.

I work well in teams and have no problem adjusting to any dynamic. I love working with people who have crazy, ambitious ideas and I love figuring out how to make ideas work. If you'd like to collaborate on anything (and I mean anything), please don't be shy to contact me!
